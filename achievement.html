Achievements of My Family

Diploma in Management [1999-2003]

During my father's service in the Royal Malaysia Police, he was offered the opportunity to pursue a Diploma. He accepted the offer and enrolled in a Diploma in Management course. At that time, my father pursued his studies through distance learning. Eventually, he completed his Diploma and graduated in 2003.

Promotion Offer by Appointment (KPSL) [2016]

In 2016, my father was promoted by appointment from Corporal to Sergeant. At that time, I felt proud of my father's achievement. To this day, he still holds the rank of Sergeant, and I hope he will be promoted to Sergeant Major.

Got 6As In SPM [2022]

In 2022, I achieved a significant milestone by scoring 6As in the SPM examination. Initially, I was not confident in myself and did not expect to achieve such excellent and meaningful results.

Special Awards in High School [2022]

In 2022, I also received 2 special awards from SMK Tawau. I was awarded the 'Anugerah Tokoh Khas PAJSK,' the 'Anugerah Tokoh HEM 2021,' and was appointed as the Head Prefect of the school.

Basic Course General Operations Force (PGA) [19-22 November 2024]

From November 19-22 2024, I participated in the Basic Course for the General Operations Force (PGA) at the Police Training Centre in Kuching. I learned a lot about the General Operations Force (PGA) and experienced a bit of 'Para Military' training within the Royal Malaysia Police.
